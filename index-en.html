Good to know

A few details so you can enjoy the day to the fullest.

👗 Dress code

For men, dark suit. For women, a wedding guest outfit that feels elegant and comfortable. We would be grateful if you could leave white and similar shades for the Bride. If you are travelling from abroad and are unsure, please do ask us.

👶 Children

This time, we would love the grown-ups to relax and enjoy the day without worries, while the little ones stay at home. Thank you very much for understanding.

👠 Footwear

The venue includes garden areas, so we would advise against very high stilettos. No alternative footwear will be provided during the party, so please keep that in mind if you would prefer to bring a change of shoes.
