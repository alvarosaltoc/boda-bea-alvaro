Accommodation

As the coach will depart from the Francesc Macià area, we are sharing a few nearby hotel suggestions in case they are helpful. Some hotels may also offer better rates if you sign up as a customer or book through their member programme.

In any case, our recommendation is simply to book whatever feels most convenient for you, depending on your plans, budget and the area of Barcelona where you would prefer to stay.

AC Hotel Irla

C/ Calvet 40-42, 08021 Barcelona. Very close to Francesc Macià, in a comfortable residential area. It may be worth checking whether there are benefits for registered members.

Vincci Mae

Avenida Diagonal, 596, 08021 Barcelona. Very well located for anyone who would like to stay especially close to the departure point. Their website mentions advantages for joining Vincci Smart.

U232 Hotel

Comte d'Urgell, 232, 08036 Barcelona. A comfortable option if you would prefer a central and well-connected area for getting around Barcelona.

Zenit Barcelona

Carrer de Santaló, 8, 08021 Barcelona. It is close to Avenida Diagonal and Plaça Francesc Macià, so it can be a very practical option.

Hesperia Presidente

Avenida Diagonal, 570, 08021 Barcelona. It has a very convenient location and their Hesperia Connection programme advertises extra benefits and member discounts.

Casual Colours Barcelona

Avinguda de Josep Tarradellas, 119-121, 08029 Barcelona. A convenient option if the area between Les Corts and Eixample suits you well.

Sunotel Aston

Carrer de París, 101-107, 08029 Barcelona. Another practical alternative in a well-connected area relatively close to Francesc Macià.

Other options

If you would rather compare more alternatives, you can also look for accommodation near Francesc Macià or in whichever part of Barcelona suits you best.
